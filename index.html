Reflect upon your own personal growth and join New York District Circle K'ers in the final District Convention of the service year! At District Convention, attendees have the opportunity to shape the future of our District by electing our new District officers! The positions open for election are District Governor, District Secretary, District Treasurer, and District Editor. They support our Circle K clubs around New York. Candidates will share their speeches and answer your questions during caucus sessions.

 

District Governor

Serves as the executive officer of the New York District. Works with the officers on the District Board to support Circle K clubs with the training, resources, and events that they need to succeed. Facilitates District Board Meetings, maintains connections with the other branches of the Kiwanis Family, and represents the District at the International level.

 

District Secretary

Serves as the direct counterpart to club secretaries, ensuring they are trained and well supported during their terms. Is the official record keeper of the District, maintaining monthly club reports and taking minutes at all District Board meetings.

 

District Treasurer

Serves as the direct counterpart to club treasurers, ensuring they are trained and well supported during their terms. Works closely with treasurers regarding International and District dues and ensures they are fundraising towards their fundraising goal. Treasurers also maintain the District budget.

 

District Editor

Serves as the direct counterpart to club editors, ensuring they are trained and well supported during their terms. Designs various District Newsletters (e.g., NYCKI News, Circling The Empire), manages the District Board’s Google Suite, maintains the District Directory, and collaborates with District Officers on projects (e.g., merchandise, resources).


If you are interested in running for one of the positions, please email District Governor Peony Tse at peony.tse@nycirclek.org and District Administrator John Keegan at johnkeegan190@gmail.com.
